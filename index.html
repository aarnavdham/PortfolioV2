[ 01 ] — Manifesto

Most of the internet is forgettable. We don't think that's an accident — we think it's a choice. We chose differently.

NVISION started in 2013 in a small apartment in Lisbon with one founder, one laptop, and one rule: every project had to be better than the last. We've grown to seven people, shipped 120+ websites and 85+ films.

We don't scale by hiring. We scale by saying no. We take on roughly twelve projects a year. The work we do take on gets our full attention for the full duration.
